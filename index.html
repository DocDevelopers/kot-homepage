Built on Family Strength

At the heart of Keep On Truckin Holdings is a family tradition of overcoming adversity and shaping new horizons. Our company’s foundation rests on the legacy of the Castellanos family — each generation driven by grit and vision.

From a time when resilience meant hard work in a garage and relentless drives on island roads, to today's pursuit of opportunity through technology and innovation, our journey is a testament to perseverance handed down, refined, and reimagined.

Generational Journey

Each chapter of the Castellanos family story adds a new layer of resilience and resourcefulness, shaping who we are today.

Porfirio Castellanos

A Visionary Builder

From humble beginnings, my grandfather began a dairy transport business, moving milk for Compañía Dominicana de Alimentos Lácteos (Codal), a Nestlé affiliate. Through sheer will and work, he built a company with over 14 trucks — a testament to grit, vision, and leadership.

Porfirio Castellanos Jr.

A Master Craftsman

Growing up in the garage where the trucks were parked, my father learned the value of dedication and mastery. As a child, he absorbed every detail of working on vehicles — evolving into a master technician, especially gifted in electrical systems. His journey embodies learning by doing and excelling through perseverance.

Porfirio Castellanos III

A Modern Storyteller

Raised in Philadelphia, I inherited the lessons of resilience and perseverance that shaped my family. Embracing new tools, I found my path in software engineering — turning family values into modern momentum, resilient always, facing forward.
