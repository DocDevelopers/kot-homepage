Three Generations of Resilience

The Foundation

It began with Porfirio Castellanos. With grit and determination, he established a dairy transportation company, hauling milk for Compañía Dominicana de Alimentos Lácteos (Codal), a subsidiary of Nestlé. Through sheer perseverance, he built his fleet to 14 trucks, setting a standard for hard work that would define the family name.

The Mechanic

His son grew up in the garage where those 14 trucks were parked. From a young boy, he learned the intricacies of engines and electrical systems, eventually becoming a master mechanic. He passed down not just the technical skill, but the resilience required to keep things moving.

The Innovation

Today, that legacy lives on in Philadelphia. The third generation—bearing the same name—has translated that mechanical mastery into the digital realm. We have evolved from fixing engines to engineering software, proving that while the tools change, the spirit of perseverance remains constant.
